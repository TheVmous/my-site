## Eagle Scout Project
- Built seven redwood picnic tables for Joshua Tree National Park
- Transported them 500 miles to [Keys Ranch](https://www.nps.gov/places/keys-ranch.htm), a historic site (I highly recommend visiting) 
- Fundraised $1200+, total volunteer hours: 517, earning Presidential Service Award (silver)
- Coordinated with National Park Service to design environment friendly and ADA compliant tables
