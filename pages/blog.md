##Keys Ranch
The NPS have done a great and very intentional job maintaining Key's Ranch. It used to be a mine until Bill Keys aquired it and it is just a marvelous site of pioneer ingenuity. He built everything from a two story house to a small dam himself!

The NPS' philosophy in preserving this is actually even more interesting: To freeze time honestly.
They recognize that every bolt they replace is a change, and eventually even that will be a part of history
